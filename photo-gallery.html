Experienced songwriter records original song for the first time

Lyric Green, an MU student studying psychology, chose to take a music composition class during her second to last semester of college. While she has played guitar since her junior year of high school and written many original songs over the years, she had no experience with production prior to enrolling in the class. One of her assignments was to write a song and record it using Logic Pro recording software to turn in for credit. She wrote and recorded almost the entire song on the weekend of Nov. 12 at Central House in Columbia. This was Green’s first time recording one of her original songs, titled “Say it Again.”

Leor Shomroni, 2022
