Six great ramen places in downtown Columbia

Now that the temperature is dropping in Columbia, it might be a good idea to check out some local restaurants that serve warm, steaming bowls of ramen. Classic Japanese-style ramen noodle soup includes a range of ingredients, but the most essential are ramen noodles, stock, scallions, and seasoned soft-boiled eggs, among many other ingredients. These six delicious options are all available for patrons ready to get something warm and tasty in their bellies, and they’re all conveniently located downtown!

Le Bao Asian Eatery

1009 Park Ave.
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 443-2867

Visit Le Bao's website

This quaint, modern-style restaurant specializing in bao offers four different types of ramen bowls, all of which are served with a boiled egg and guaranteed to leave you feeling full and satisfied.

Kampai Alley

907 Alley A
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 442-2239

Visit Kampai Alley's website

This upscale, classy restaurant tucked away in a downtown alley features a classic Japanese-style ramen bowl with a rich, aromatic stock, scattered sprouts, green onions and spinach. To top it off, two slices of juicy roast pork.

Sake Japanese Bistro and Bar

16 S 10th St.
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 443-7253

Visit Sake's website

This stylistic restaurant recently under new management features a dish that is not a traditional ramen bowl, as it has rice noodles instead of the traditional ramen noodles. They also add jalapeños and raw carrots, which also stray from tradition but a little extra vegetables and spice never hurt anyone!

Big Mama Chim's Noodle House

905 Alley A
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 777-3108

Visit Big Mama Chim's website

Enjoy a warm, welcoming atmosphere and balanced flavors at this humble but delicious Thai restaurant. While they don’t technically have ramen soup, they offer multiple delicious noodle soup dishes that will warm you up and leave you wanting more. Also, their 1-10 rating system for spiciness allows you full control over how hot you want your food!

Thai Express

308 S 9th St. Suite 107
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 442-3998

Visit Thai Express' website

Experience a surprisingly large range of options at this quaint Thai restaurant located at the heart of downtown Columbia, where their lunch combos make it a perfect spot for your lunch break. They offer three noodle soup dishes that include rice noodles, bok choy, bean sprouts, green onions and garlic oil topped with cilantro.

U Knead Sweets Bakery

808 Cherry St.
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 777-8808

Visit U Knead Sweets' website

This traditional Chinese bakery specializes in delicious cakes but also serves Chinese noodle soups. They offer a beef noodle soup with tender, marbled beef slices and a chili oil soup that is guaranteed to make you sweat.
